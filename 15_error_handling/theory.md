## Variants
- **FileNotFound**
- **SyntaxError** (mis-spelt word, too many brackets, or missing comma)
- **KeyError** (non-existent key used)
- **NameError** (variable with a name not recognised, misspelling)
- **ZeroDivisionError** (when you try to divide by zero or something mathematically impossible)
- **IndexError** (non-existent index in a list)
- **TypeError** (different data types attempting to combine something together like string and integer)
- **ValueError** (something has the wrong error)
- **RuntimeError** (most general things)
